About Us

FlashPoint is designed to bring fun and interactive learning to groups. Our platform makes it easy to create custom quizzes and challenge your friends or team members, fostering a dynamic and engaging environment.

How It Works

1. Setup Players

Enter names for up to six players to kick off your quiz session.

2. Create Questions

Each player takes a turn creating five questions for their peers.

3. Quiz Time!

Answer questions in a fast-paced, timed environment and score points.
